About

Howdy! Thanks for visiting my page. I commonly go by Sasa. I'm originally from Indonesia but have been living in the States for the past six years. Prior to my recent move to New York in August, I worked for two years as a data analyst, where I found my passion for solving problems with datasets and statistical techniques.

My expertise and interests include machine learning, predictive modeling, data visualizations, business intelligence, and cross-functional teamwork. I also get really excited when it comes to data for good. One of my favorite highlights this past fall was winning the 2016 Impact Hackathon by Columbia Business School.

When I'm not playing with data, I enjoy learning new things, spending quality time with loved ones, and constantly thinking about ways to change the world. My thirst for adventures has taken me to over twenty countries worldwide, but New York City and Interlaken still got to be my most favorite place to date.
