About

Holla! Thanks for visiting my webpage. I'm Yosia, but more often go by Bewok. I was born and raised in Jakarta, a metropolitan city in Indonesia known for it's traffic jam and flood. As adventurous as Jakarta made me to be, I've started living on my own when I was 17. I have since traveled to many places in Indonesia (though Yogyakarta and Bali are still my favorite place to vocation).

Loving my first part-time job, I decided to pursue data for my long-term career.

My expertise and interests include machine learning, predictive modeling, data visualizations, business intelligence, and cross-functional teamwork. I also get really excited when it comes to data for good. One of my favorite highlights this past fall was attended online course from Stanford and UC Berkeley.

When I'm not playing with data, I enjoy learning new things, spending quality time with loved ones, and constantly thinking about ways to change the world.
